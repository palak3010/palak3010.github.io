Hi, I'm Palak — a web developer who's spent the last four years deep in backend systems: booking engines, multi-vendor marketplaces, payment integrations, and the APIs that hold it all together.

I like the unglamorous half of a product — the part where a booking has to lock correctly, a refund has to actually reach the customer, and a driver's ETA has to update in real time. Most of my work has been in PHP and Laravel, alongside JavaScript and MySQL, building for e-commerce and on-demand platforms.

I've also led small development teams and stayed close to clients directly, so requirements don't get lost between a call and a commit — and I use AI-assisted tools where they genuinely speed things up, while keeping architecture and logic decisions my own.
